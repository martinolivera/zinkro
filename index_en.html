Welcome to Zinkro

Zinkro is an alternative calendar proposing 13 months of 28 days (4 exact weeks each), plus one or two days out of time, starting on March 21st of the year -10,000 (Holocene Era). Its goal is to reconnect human time with natural and cosmic rhythms, simplify annual planning, and democratize month symbolism.

  • Perfect symmetry: each month starts on the same weekday.
  • Months of equal length, no irregular adjustments.
  • Starts at the March equinox (natural renewal).
  • Days out of time for global reflection and celebration.
  • Flexible month naming: numeric, natural, human qualities, ancestral cultures.
  • Leap year rule: compatible with the Gregorian cycle.
